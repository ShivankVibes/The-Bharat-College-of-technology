      How to play

      Rules & Hints

      The idea

      Over twelve years you grow one rented tuition room into an empire. Every choice decides whether it grows honestly or by capturing the exam system — and who pays for it.

      Your numbers

      • Treasury (Mudra) — your money. It ticks in automatically from student fees, even while you're away.
      • Income/s & Students — fees come from enrolled students; more students, more income.
      • Saakh — your credibility. Slow to build, easy to burn, impossible to buy back.
      • Power — your reach and influence.
      • Heat — scandal exposure. Let it get too high and the empire gets raided.
      • The human ledger — Aarav, Meena and the Sharmas. They are real people your choices land on. Keep an eye on them.

      What you do

      • Build — spend Mudra on subject rooms, floors, branches and staff to grow students and income. Tap “Show what each upgrade does” to see the exact effect.
      • Open files — story chapters unlock as your enrolment grows. Open one to make a moral choice that hits both your stats and your economy.
      • Schemes — corrupt choices unlock schemes that boost income, but raise Heat and grind down the people who trust you, every second they run.

      Hints

      • Build subject rooms and a Marketing floor first to fill seats and unlock the next file.
      • Ad blitz instantly fills most empty seats — great right after you expand capacity.
      • Counselors lower Heat and protect your students — they can avert the darkest outcome even on a corrupt run.
      • Teachers raise Saakh; the Finance floor squeezes more fee from every student.
      • Endings judge the character of your empire, not your bank balance. Money never buys you out.
